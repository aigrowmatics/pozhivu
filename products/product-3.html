Imagine standing in a pine forest at dawn — that's the experience our Forest Blend brings to your home. Each oil is sourced from wild or organically farmed plants and steam-distilled to capture their full therapeutic profile. Add 5–8 drops to a diffuser to transform any room into a woodland sanctuary.

  1. Diffuser: Add 5–8 drops with water. Run for 30–60 min intervals.
  2. Bath: Mix 6 drops with 1 tbsp carrier oil before adding to bath water.
  3. Massage: Dilute 2–3 drops in 10ml coconut or jojoba oil.
  4. Inhale: Apply 1 drop to palms, rub, cup over nose and breathe deeply.

Never apply undiluted to skin. Keep away from children and eyes.
