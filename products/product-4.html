Our Green Clay Face Mask is a weekly ritual for clear, balanced skin. The mineral-rich French Green Clay draws out impurities and excess sebum, while Aloe Vera soothes any potential irritation. Tea Tree provides a gentle antibacterial action, making it ideal for oily and breakout-prone skin types.

  1. Cleanse your face with a gentle cleanser.
  2. Apply a generous, even layer to face and neck, avoiding eyes.
  3. Leave on for 10–15 minutes (or until dry).
  4. Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water.
  5. Follow with your favourite moisturiser or serum.
  6. Use 1–2 times per week for best results.
