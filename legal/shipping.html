2. Our Shipping Partners

We work with India's most trusted logistics providers to ensure your order arrives safely and on time. The partner assigned to your order depends on your pin code and selected shipping method.

🚚 Delhivery

India's largest logistics company covering 18,000+ pin codes. Real-time tracking via SMS and Delhivery app.

Track: delhivery.com/tracking

⚡ BlueDart

Premium express delivery partner for time-sensitive shipments. Covers 35,000+ locations across India.

Track: bluedart.com/tracking

📦 DTDC

Reliable courier partner for standard deliveries. Network of 10,500+ offices across India including tier-2 and tier-3 cities.

Track: dtdc.in/tracking

🏃 Dunzo / Swiggy Genie

Hyperlocal same-day delivery for Bengaluru customers. Live tracking in real-time via the partner app.

Available in Bengaluru only. Orders before 12 PM.

🌍 Ecom Express

End-to-end logistics for e-commerce. Specialised in eco-friendly packaging and last-mile delivery.

📮 India Post

For remote and rural pin codes not serviceable by private couriers. Available across all 1.5 lakh+ post offices in India.

Track: indiapost.gov.in

3. Packaging

We are committed to sustainable packaging. All GreenLeaf orders are shipped in:

  • Recycled kraft cardboard boxes — 100% recyclable
  • Compostable void fill (cornstarch peanuts or kraft paper — no styrofoam)
  • Biodegradable bubble wrap where fragile item protection is needed
  • Soy-based ink printed shipping labels

Please break down and recycle your box after unpacking. Our #ZeroWaste initiative means we aim for 100% plastic-free packaging by 2026.

4. Order Processing Time

Orders are processed and dispatched within 24 hours on business days (Monday to Saturday, excluding public holidays). Orders placed on Sundays or public holidays are dispatched the next business day. You will receive a dispatch confirmation email with a tracking link once your order is shipped.

5. Tracking Your Order

After dispatch, you'll receive:

  • An email with your AWB (tracking) number and a direct tracking link
  • An SMS to your registered mobile number with tracking details
  • Delivery status updates at key milestones (dispatched, out for delivery, delivered)

6. Failed Delivery & Undeliverable Orders

If delivery fails (recipient unavailable, incorrect address, etc.), our partner will make up to 3 delivery attempts. After that, the order is returned to our warehouse. We will contact you to arrange re-delivery (additional charges may apply) or issue a refund minus original shipping cost.

Please ensure your shipping address and phone number are accurate to avoid delivery issues.

7. International Shipping

Currently, GreenLeaf Store ships within India only. We are working on international shipping and expect to launch it by Q3 2025. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified.
