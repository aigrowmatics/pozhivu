GreenLeaf Store ("we", "our", "us") operates the website greenleaf.store. We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy in accordance with the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India).

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account, place an order, or contact us, we collect:

  • Full name, email address, phone number
  • Billing and shipping address
  • Payment information (processed securely via Razorpay — we do not store card details)
  • Account credentials (password stored as hashed value)
  • Messages and communications you send us

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

When you visit our website, we automatically receive:

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type, operating system, device information
  • Pages visited, time on site, referral URL
  • Cookie identifiers and session data

1.3 Data from Third Parties

If you sign in via Google or Facebook, we receive your name, email, and profile picture from those services, subject to your privacy settings on those platforms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data to:

  • Process and fulfil your orders and send order confirmations
  • Send shipping updates and tracking information via our logistics partners
  • Provide customer support and respond to your queries
  • Send promotional emails and offers (only with your consent — unsubscribe anytime)
  • Improve our website, products, and user experience
  • Detect and prevent fraud and unauthorised access
  • Comply with legal obligations under Indian law

Legal basis: We process data under contract performance (order fulfilment), legitimate interests (fraud prevention), legal obligation, and consent (marketing).

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with trusted parties necessary to run our business:

  • Shipping Partners — Delhivery, BlueDart, DTDC, Ecom Express, Dunzo — receive your name, phone, and delivery address to complete delivery.
  • Payment Processors — Razorpay processes payments; we receive only a transaction ID and status. Your card/UPI details are never stored on our servers.
  • Analytics — Google Analytics (anonymised) to understand site traffic.
  • Legal Requirements — We may disclose your data if required by law, court order, or government authority.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in during your session
  • Remember your cart and preferences
  • Analyse website traffic (Google Analytics)
  • Serve relevant advertisements (if opted in)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality such as cart persistence.

5. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures including:

  • 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Passwords stored using bcrypt hashing (never in plain text)
  • Payment data processed through PCI-DSS compliant Razorpay gateway
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Strict access controls — only authorised personnel can access customer data

Despite best efforts, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to contact us immediately if you suspect unauthorised access.

6. Your Rights

Under the DPDP Act 2023 and applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt-out — unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
  • Data Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Withdraw Consent — withdraw consent for processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@greenleaf.store. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account data: Until account deletion request + 90 days
  • Order records: 7 years (required under Indian tax law)
  • Marketing data: Until you unsubscribe
  • Security logs: 12 months

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to persons under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related queries, complaints, or to exercise your rights:
