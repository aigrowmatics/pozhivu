Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2025 · We want you to love every purchase.

🌿 Quick Summary

📅
30 Days

Return window from delivery date

💸
Full Refund

For eligible returns, within 7–10 days

🚚
Free Pickup

We arrange reverse pickup at no cost

1. Return Eligibility

We accept returns within 30 days of the delivery date for items that meet the following conditions:

  • Item is unused, unopened, and in its original packaging
  • Seal / shrink-wrap is intact and undamaged
  • Item is not on the non-returnable list below
  • Return request is raised within the 30-day window

2. Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene, safety, and quality reasons, the following categories are not eligible for return:

  • Opened or used skincare products (serums, masks, creams)
  • Opened herbal teas, food, or nutritional supplements
  • Essential oils with broken seals
  • Products marked "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable" on the product page
  • Customised or personalised products
  • Gift cards and digital vouchers

If you received a damaged, defective, or wrong item, we will accept a return regardless of the above conditions. Please report such issues within 48 hours of delivery with photographs.

3. How to Initiate a Return

Step 1: Email returns@greenleaf.store with your order ID, the item(s) you wish to return, and the reason for return.

Step 2: Our team will review and approve the request within 1–2 business days.

Step 3: We will schedule a free reverse pickup via our logistics partner from your delivery address.

Step 4: Once we receive and inspect the item, we will process your refund within 3–5 business days.

4. Refund Process

Refunds are processed to the original payment method:

  • UPI / Net Banking / Card: Refund within 7–10 business days after item receipt and inspection
  • Cash on Delivery: Refund via bank transfer (NEFT) — we'll request your bank details; allow 7–10 days
  • GreenLeaf Store Credit: Instant credit to your account (optional — choose this for faster resolution)

Shipping charges (if paid) are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (wrong / damaged item).

5. Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you'd like a different product, please initiate a return for the original item and place a new order. If you received a wrong item, we will ship the correct one free of charge after confirming the return.

6. Damaged or Defective Items

If your order arrives damaged, please:

  • Take clear photographs of the damaged packaging and product within 48 hours of delivery
  • Email returns@greenleaf.store with your order ID and photos
  • Do not discard the packaging — it may be required for our shipping partner's claim

We will arrange a full replacement or refund at no additional cost to you.

7. Contact

For returns, refunds, or any order issues: returns@greenleaf.store or call +91 800 123 4567 (Mon–Fri, 10am–6pm IST).
