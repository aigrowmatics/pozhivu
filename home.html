Our Story

We believe nature knows best.

Founded in 2019, GreenLeaf started with a simple belief: that what you put on and in your body matters — and so does what you leave behind on the planet.

Every product we sell is carefully sourced, certified organic, and packaged in recyclable or compostable materials. We partner directly with Indian farmers and artisans to ensure fair trade and traceability.

🌍 For every order placed, we plant a tree through our #GreenEarth initiative.
